5 Pillars

Islam

Numbah 1

There is no God but Allah(SWT) and Muhammad(SAW) is His Prophet

Numbah 2

Pray five times a day.

Numbah 3

Fast the holy month of Ramadan if you are in good health

Numbah 4

If financially sound, must pay a certain percentage from your income as Zakat

Numbah 5

Must perform Hajj if financially sound.
